## Full description (Play Store, EN)

Forget numbers. Think in runes.

Runic Sudoku is a fantasy logic puzzle game built on classic sudoku deduction — but instead of digits, you place six ancient rune symbols on a 6×6 stone grid. Each row, column, and carved section must contain every rune exactly once.

Simple rules. Surprisingly deep.

**HOW IT WORKS**

Each puzzle gives you a grid with some runes already placed. Your task: fill in the rest using pure logic. No guessing. Every solution can be deduced step by step.

- Tap a cell, tap a rune, done.
- Use Notes mode to mark candidates in any cell.
- Stuck? One free Check per puzzle reveals mistakes. Need more help? A Hint shows your next logical step.

**FOUR CHAPTERS. ONE JOURNEY.**

Progress through the Rune Trials — four chapters of increasing depth:

🪨 Quick Runes — short puzzles for a spare minute
📜 Normal Seals — balanced challenges that reward focus
💎 Tricky Glyphs — puzzles that demand careful deduction
🌑 Deep Chambers — the hardest runes, for those who seek them

Complete puzzles to unlock the next. Each chapter has its own atmosphere — stone halls, ancient archives, crystal caves, arcane depths.

**DAILY PUZZLE**

A new rune puzzle every day. Track your streak. Come back tomorrow.

**CLEAN. FAIR. NO TRICKS.**

- No energy bars. No lives. No forced timers.
- Ads only between puzzles — never during active play.
- Remove Ads available as a one-time purchase.
- Hints and mistake checks are optional, not forced.
- Plays fully offline.

**100 HAND-TUNED PUZZLES**

Every puzzle is generated and scored for difficulty. Quick puzzles are genuinely quick. Deep puzzles are genuinely deep. No padding, no filler.
